Agriculture has turned out to be an economic booster contributing to the economic development of many countries over the many few decades. As we know that agriculture is considered the backbone of Pakistan's economy, which relies heavily on its major crops. The agricultural industry on Pakistan is on manual system which heavily effect on farmers and agriculture productions. I have designed a system to provide the best farming products to the farmers. The system is a web-based application. The system has several modules a search platform where a farmer can find their agricultural products at fewer prices in discount rate in every product than in market rate according to their choices. This system also helps the farmer that how much amount of synthetic chemicals product is used per square feet to the crops and also mentions which type of diseases of plants for that product is used. The objective of this project is to help a farmer
